How it works

Uses your browser's built-in Speech Recognition engine. No server, no API key, no cost. Everything runs locally on your device.

Keyboard shortcut

Press Ctrl + Shift + M to toggle recording on/off.

Auto-restart

Chrome stops recognition every ~5-10 min. Vocalize auto-restarts and marks boundaries with · · · so you can see where words might be missing.

Auto-save

Your transcript is saved to browser storage every 5 seconds. If you close the tab by accident, it's restored next time you open the page.
